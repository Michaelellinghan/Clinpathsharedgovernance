Coagulation

Investigating the delicate balance between bleeding and clotting.

The Clotting Cascade

Coagulation is your body's vital process for stopping bleeding. It involves a complex chain reaction of proteins called clotting factors. Our job is to test how well this system is working, which is crucial for surgery, monitoring blood-thinning medications, and investigating bleeding or clotting disorders.
