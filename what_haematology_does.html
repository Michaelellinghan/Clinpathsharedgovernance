What is Haematology?

Haematology is the branch of medicine all about your blood and the bits of your body that make it, like your bone marrow. When your doctor sends a blood sample to the lab, it's our team of 'blood detectives' who analyse it to find clues about your health.

Our work ranges from the routine Full Blood Count, which gives a vital snapshot of a patient's health, to complex investigations into bleeding disorders and blood cancers. Explore our pages to learn more about what we do.
