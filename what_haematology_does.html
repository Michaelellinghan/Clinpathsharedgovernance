The Core of Haematology

The Full Blood Count (FBC)

This is our number one test! Think of it as a census of your blood, counting every red cell, white cell, and platelet. It gives us a vital snapshot of your health, helping to spot everything from simple anaemia to serious diseases like leukaemia.

See what the FBC tells us →

Coagulation: The Clotting Experts

Ever wonder how a papercut stops bleeding? That's coagulation! We investigate the complex system of proteins that form clots. We help diagnose bleeding problems and ensure patients on blood thinners like warfarin are on the perfect dose.

Unravel the clotting cascade →

Blood Films: Under the Microscope

Sometimes, you need the human eye. Our scientists are experts at looking at blood on a glass slide. We can spot oddly shaped cells, signs of infection, and even parasites like malaria that our analysers can't see. It's a critical skill that saves lives.
