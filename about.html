About the Council

We are a team of passionate laboratory staff dedicated to improving our workplace and showcasing the vital role of clinical pathology.

Our Mission

Our Shared Governance Council is a staff-led body that aims to empower all members of the Clinical Pathology department. We work collaboratively to identify opportunities for improvement, launch innovative projects, and foster a positive, engaging, and supportive work environment. We are the bridge between staff and management, ensuring every voice is heard.
