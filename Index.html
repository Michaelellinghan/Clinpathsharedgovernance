Our Projects

Patient Tours & Stories

Giving patients a behind-the-scenes look at our labs and sharing their powerful stories helps to demystify the process, build trust, and highlight the human element at the heart of our work. Read one such story from a long-term dialysis patient below.

Laboratory Video Tours

We're curating a series of videos for each department in Clinical Pathology to teach the wards and GPs what we do and what they can do to help us improve patient care. These short, engaging videos will cover key aspects of the pathology process, from sample collection to result interpretation, providing a valuable educational resource for clinical staff.

Videos Coming Soon!

Garden Project

We are developing a calming green space for staff and patients to relax and recuperate. Work has already started, and we've collected many wonderful plants, thanks to the generosity of Nottcutts in Nottingham and Six Acres Nursery in Leicester. We've also received pots kindly donated by Maria Pallon.

Funding has been secured for new furniture, and we plan on sprucing up and refreshing the current items. Keep an eye out for updates on a big Pathology garden sort-and-clean lunch day!

A Gentle Reminder:

Our new plants need your help! If you are passing by and see that they look a little dry, please be so kind as to give them a quick water. Thank you!
