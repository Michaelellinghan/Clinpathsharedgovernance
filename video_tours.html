Laboratory Video Tours & Guides

We're curating a series of videos and guides for each department in Clinical Pathology to teach the wards and GPs what we do and what they can do to help us improve patient care. These short, engaging resources will cover key aspects of the pathology process, from sample collection to result interpretation, providing a valuable educational resource for clinical staff.

What Does Haematology Do?

Explore the world of blood cells, from the Full Blood Count to the art of morphology.

What Does Clinical Chemistry Do?

A visual guide to the journey of a sample through the chemistry lab, from receipt to result.

What Does Transfusion Do?

Coming Soon! A guide to blood groups, antibodies, and ensuring safe transfusions.
