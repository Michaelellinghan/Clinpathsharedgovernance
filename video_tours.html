The Lab Tours Project

Bringing the laboratory to you through engaging and educational video tours.

Our Model for Improvement

What are we trying to accomplish?

To demystify the work of a clinical pathology lab, making it accessible and understandable for staff, students, and the public, thereby increasing engagement and appreciation for our profession.

How will we know a change is an improvement?

Success will be measured through website traffic to the tour pages, positive feedback from viewers, and its use as a successful training and induction tool for new staff.

What change can we make?

By creating a central hub of in-depth, department-specific pages and videos, we can provide a permanent, high-quality educational resource that showcases our work.
