Liver Function Tests (LFTs)

LFTs are another very common set of tests. They help doctors assess the function of the liver and can indicate if there is any inflammation or damage.

What We Test

The LFT panel includes Bilirubin, Alkaline Phosphatase (ALP), Alanine Transaminase (ALT), Aspartate Aminotransferase (AST), Gamma-Glutamyl Transferase (GGT), Total Protein, and Albumin.

How We Test

All LFTs use a method called Photometry. This involves passing a beam of light through the sample after a chemical reaction has occurred. The amount of light that is absorbed by the sample tells us the concentration of the substance we are measuring.

Process Pathway

Analysis: ~10 minutes per test
Like U&Es, the automated process means times are not cumulative.
