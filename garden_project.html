The Pathology Garden Project

Creating a green space for staff to relax, recharge, and connect.

Our Model for Improvement

What are we trying to accomplish?

To create an accessible and calming green space that improves staff wellbeing by providing a dedicated place to relax and recuperate during breaks.

How will we know a change is an improvement?

Success will be measured by staff usage of the area, positive feedback from colleagues, and the continued physical improvement and upkeep of the garden space.

What change can we make?

By securing funding, purchasing furniture, obtaining plant donations, and organizing staff volunteer days, we can transform an unused area into a thriving garden.

An Update on our Garden Project!

We are thrilled to share some fantastic progress on our project to create a calming green space for staff to relax and recuperate. Thanks to the funding we secured, the very first piece of our new garden furniture has arrived!

Looking After Our New Furniture

Please help us keep the new cushions in great condition. If it looks like it's about to rain, we would be very grateful if you could bring them inside. We are looking into getting a garden box and cover to make this easier for everyone.

A Gentle Reminder for Our Plants

Our plants still need your help too! If you are passing by and see that they look a little dry, please be so kind as to give them a quick water. Thank you!

We'll continue to keep you updated, and don't forget to keep an eye out for news on a big Pathology garden sort-and-clean lunch day!
