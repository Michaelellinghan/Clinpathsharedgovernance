The Patient Behind the Sample

Sharing the stories of the lives we impact every day.

Why This Project is Important

"It's easy to forget that there's a patient behind every sample tube we receive in the laboratory. Knowing why every single sample is important, and putting faces to them, will hopefully improve our role in patient care."

Our Model for Improvement

What are we trying to accomplish? (Aim)

To reconnect laboratory staff with the human impact of their work, fostering a stronger sense of purpose and improving our collective role in patient care.

How will we know a change is an improvement? (Measure)

We will gather qualitative feedback from staff and share anecdotal evidence of increased engagement and motivation, demonstrating a positive shift in our workplace culture.

What change can we make? (Change Idea)

By actively conducting, sharing, and promoting interviews with patients, we can make their stories a visible and integral part of our departmental identity.
