The Clinical Chemistry Digest

Welcome to Clinical Chemistry! Here, we test what's dissolved in your bloodstream rather than the blood cells themselves. We spin the blood down to separate the watery serum, which contains all the interesting chemicals. Then, using a variety of clever reactions, we uncover vital clues about your health!
