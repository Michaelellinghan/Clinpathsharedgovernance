Specialist Chemistry

This is where we delve deeper into the body's complex chemistry, using advanced techniques to diagnose and monitor a wide range of conditions.

What We Do

The Specialist Chemistry department covers several key areas:

  • Toxicology: Detecting and measuring drugs and other toxins in the body.
  • Endocrinology: The study of hormones and their effects.
  • Inherited Metabolic Diseases (IMD): Diagnosing rare genetic disorders that affect the body's metabolism.

How We Test

We use a highly sophisticated technique called Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (LCMS).

This powerful method allows us to separate complex mixtures into their individual components and then identify and quantify each one with incredible accuracy. It's essential for detecting very low levels of substances, which is crucial in toxicology and endocrinology.

Process Pathway

Due to the complexity of these tests and the technology involved, the turnaround time is longer than for routine tests. It typically takes around 1 to 2 weeks to get results from Specialist Chemistry.
