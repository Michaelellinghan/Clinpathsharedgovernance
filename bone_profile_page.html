Bone Profile

The Bone Profile is a group of tests used to screen for diseases of the bone. It gives a good overall picture of bone health.

What We Test

A bone profile includes tests for Alkaline Phosphatase (ALP), Albumin, Calcium, and Phosphate.

How We Test

All the tests in a bone profile use the Photometry method, just like LFTs. A chemical reaction causes a colour change in the sample, and we measure the absorption of light to find the result.

Process Pathway

Booking In & Processing: The time is the same as for other basic chemistry, around 41 minutes total.

Analysis: ~10 minutes per test
The automated process allows for rapid, non-cumulative analysis times.

Total Run Time: ~55 minutes
