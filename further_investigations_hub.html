Specialist Areas

Beyond the routine Full Blood Count and Group & Screen, our laboratory performs a wide range of specialised tests to diagnose complex conditions. These investigations are grouped into three main specialist areas. Click on a section to learn more.

Red Cell Investigations

Learn about screening for inherited red cell disorders like Sickle Cell Disease and Thalassaemia, and how we hunt for blood parasites like Malaria.

Haemostasis & Thrombophilia

Explore our in-depth investigations into the clotting system, diagnosing both bleeding disorders and conditions that cause excessive clotting.

Antenatal Screening

Discover the vital tests we perform during pregnancy to protect the health of both mother and baby, including Anti-D prophylaxis.
